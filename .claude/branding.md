## Visual Style
- **Colors**: Golden (#D4A843, #F7D96E), purple (#7B4FBF), dark backgrounds (#06050E, #121024)
- **Typography**: Cinzel (headings), Philosopher (body)
- **Tone**: Premium, esoteric, futuristic, mystical
- **Format**: SVG for logo/icons, PNG only for social media assets
- **Favicon**: Symbol only (no text), recognizable at 16x16px

## Rules
- Logo must work on dark backgrounds
- Keep symbol simple enough to recognize when blurred
- Golden strokes, glowing effects, constellation motifs
- No cluttered designs — clean and minimal
